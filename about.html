ABOUT SKIPCLASS

SkipClass is a free, unblocked games website built for students and anyone who just wants to play some games without hassle. No sign up, no downloads, no paywalls — just games.

We host HTML5 and Flash (via Ruffle) games that run entirely in your browser. Every game on this site is self-hosted, meaning we don't rely on third-party game sites that might be blocked on your network.

SkipClass is a passion project. We're constantly adding new games and improving the site based on player feedback. Got a game you want to see? Use the suggest button on the main page!
